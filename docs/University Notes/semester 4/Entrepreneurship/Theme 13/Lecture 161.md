# Historical Balance Sheet
## Assets
Assets = liabilities + shareholder's equity

There are 2 types of assets

### Current
- accounts receivables
- inventories

### Fixed
- land
- buildings and equipment

## Liabilities
### Current
- accounts payable

### Long Term
- long term debts

## Shareholder's Equity
- Common stock
- retained earnings
