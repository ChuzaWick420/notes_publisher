# Benefits of Franchising:
1. You get proper training.
2. Financial assistance.
3. Proven market methods.
4. Managerial assistance.
5. Lower risk rate.

# Negatives of Franchising:
1. Franchise fees.
2. Royalties.
3. Restrictions on growth.
4. Less independent in operations.
5. Franchisor might be the sole supplier of the supplies.
6. Can be terminated whenever franchisor wants.
